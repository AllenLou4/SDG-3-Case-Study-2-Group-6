Ideation Document: SDG 3 Good Health and Well-Being

Application: SPROUT - An app for nutritionists to communicate for the well-being of patients

Introduction and Overview:

Health care is a necessity, including dieting and monitoring calorie intake for well-being. Traditional in-person consultations with nutritionists can be challenging due to traffic and busy schedules. SPROUT addresses this by providing a platform for convenient online communication between patients and nutritionists.

Problem Statement on SDG 3 - NutriApp:

Due to a lack of access to professional nutrition advice, people often resort to unreliable information, leading to health issues. NutriApp focuses on providing verified nutrition information and consultations within Cebu.

Scopes:

  • In-app messaging with a nutritionist
  • On-app verified newsfeed articles
  • Selection of nutritionist/dietitian based on user's needs
  • User and nutritionist profiling

Ideation and Brainstorming:

For ideation and brainstorming of ideas, we used SCAMPER ideation, and for identifying problems and causes, we used a fishbone diagram.

SCAMPER Ideation

  • Substitute:

    • Integrate a goal-setting feature within the app, allowing users to set and track specific health and nutrition goals alongside their consultations.

    • Offer gamified fitness challenges and activities.

    • Use AI-powered chat-bots or virtual assistants for basic guidance and support.

  • Combine:

    • Partner with licensed nutritionist

    • Integrate the system with grocery delivery services for convenient access to healthy food options.

    • Partner with FDA for compliance and risk mitigation

  • Adapt:

    • App creates convenience to meet a professional in the comfort of their time

    • Adapt the system to specific dietary needs (e.g., allergies, intolerance, cultural preferences).

    • App will service the user like a face to face consulting

  • Modify:

    • Modify the app to include a feature that tracks and magnifies the progress of the user's health metrics over time.

    • Use social media integration to encourage peer support and accountability.

    • Gamify the system to make healthy eating and exercise more engaging and motivating.

  • Put to Other Use:

    • Offer the system to schools and workplaces to promote healthy lifestyles.

    • Use to promote good health practices near you

  • Eliminate:

    • Reduce the need to go to the clinic for nutritional tips

    • Eliminate questionable sources of information relating to nutrition

    • Eliminate the need for manual data entry by integrating with other health and fitness apps.

    • Reduce the cost of the system by using open-source software resources.

  • Reverse:

    • The nutritionist can also have an account as a user that needs of service

    • Offer rewards for positive behavior changes instead of punishments for unhealthy choices.

Fishbone Diagram

Problem

How can we provide people with the best health advice through your phone?

  • People:

    Expertise
    • Are the health advisors qualified and have relevant experience?
    • Are there different specialties available (e.g., nutrition, fitness, mental health)?
    • How is their knowledge continuously updated?
    Proposed Solution:
    • Partner with accredited and licensed healthcare professionals: Ensure advisors have relevant experience and qualifications in nutrition, fitness, or mental health depending on their focus.
    • Establish a clear vetting process: Evaluate advisors' credentials, experience, and communications skills.
    • Offer ongoing training and development: Keep advisors updated on the latest evidence-based practices and trends.
    Availability:
    • Are advisors available when users need them (e.g., 24/7 live chat, scheduled appointments)?
    • Are there different language options available?
    Proposed Solution
    • Offer a variety of communication channels: Live chat, messaging, scheduled calls, or a combination depending on user preferences and urgency.
    • Consider language options: Cater to a wider audience by providing advisors fluent in multiple languages.
    • Set clear response times: Establish and communicate reasonable wait times for different communication channels.

  • Process

    Monitoring and Follow-up
    • Does the system track progress towards goals?
    • Does it offer reminders and motivational messages?
    • Does it allow for adjustments to the plan based on feedback?
    Proposed Solution:
    • Notification system for both parties (nutritionist and client)
    • Adjustments and feedback: Allow users to provide feedback and enable advisors to modify plans based on progress.
    • Track progress: Offer progress reports and visualizations to motivate users and measure effectiveness.
    Mode of Payment Proposed Solution:
    • Gcash
    • Cash
    • Card
    How can two users communicate/ interact? Proposed Solution:
    • App Chat / Call
    Fair Distributing of Clients for Nutritionist
    • Algorithm that automatically balance out the distributing of clients

  • User Experience

    • How can I find trusted nutritionists?
    • Are there available nutritionists at this specific time?
    • Can this nutritionist be trusted?
    • Not tech-savvy
    Proposed Solution:
    • Shows top nutritionists based on reviews and ratings
    • Can sort the nutritionists based on time availability and medical expertise
    • On boarding tutorial
    • Make the UI and flow of the app more intuitive (Icons, colors and all)
    • Have an option or button to review the tutorial

  • Pricing

    • Wanting the best advice for a cheap price:
    • Proposed Solution:
    • Prices can be sorted by price
    • AI chatbot for common questions
    Wanting to negotiate on the price of consultation: Proposed Solution:
    • Negotiable prices are dependent on the nutritionist's specifications
    • Are there different tiers with varying features and benefits?
    • Are there free trials or introductory offers?
    • Is the pricing transparent and easy to understand?

    • Transparent pricing: Clearly communicate pricing details, including subscription length, renewal terms, and any hidden fees.
    • Free trials: Provide introductory periods to allow users to experience the app before committing.

  • Does the app foster a supportive and inclusive community?
  • Does it offer opportunities for users to connect and share experiences?
  • Does it promote positive and realistic goals?
  • Proposed Solution:
    • Create forums and groups: Enable users to connect, share experiences, and build support networks.
    • Partner with health organizations: Collaborate with organizations promoting healthy and sustainable living to reach wider audiences.
    • Promote positive health goals: Focus on realistic, achievable goals that emphasize well-being over aesthetics.
